What it does

Everything stays one shake away.

Shake to summon

Start dragging anything, give your cursor a quick shake, and the shelf slides in right where you are.

Grab from Finder

Select items in Finder and press Option and Tab to send them straight to the shelf.

Always on top

Once open, the shelf stays above every other window until you close it.

Take one or take all

Drag a single row out, or pull the whole stack at once from the footer.

Drag out anywhere

Drop items into any app or folder, exactly like dragging from Finder.

Copy, move, or zip

Act on everything on the shelf from the footer: copy somewhere, move it, or bundle it into a single zip.

Three moves, no window juggling.

  1. 1

    Drag

    Start dragging any files, from Finder or any other app.

  2. 2

    Shake

    Wiggle your cursor. The shelf appears and you drop everything in.

  3. 3

    Drop

    Later, drag items out to their destination, one by one or all together.

No file-type limits.

If you can drag it, the shelf can hold it.

Get started

Up and running in a minute.

  1. Download the latest ShelfDrop-macos.zip.
  2. Unzip it and move ShelfDrop.app into Applications.
  3. On first launch, Control-click the app and choose Open.

First-launch warning

Builds are ad-hoc signed, not notarized, so macOS may warn the first time. If you see "ShelfDrop is damaged," clear the quarantine flag:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/ShelfDrop.app

Already installed? Update from the terminal:

curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/hayashiii-ghub/shelfdrop/main/script/install_latest.sh | bash
